Rohan Padmakumar

Rohan is an actor, director, and writer, currently represented by Prestigious Powerhouse, working in both Theatre and Film in New York City.

More

About Rohan

Rohan began acting at the age of 12 with an abridged performance of Shakespeare's "A Midsummer Night's Dream." Throughout middle and high school, he acted in various stage plays, both classical and contemporary, before branching out into film in his senior year of high school with a lead role in the AAHSFF "Best Screenplay" Award winning short, "Supplementary," which he co-wrote with director Kelly Yu.

Sarah Lawrence College

Rohan continued his schooling at Sarah Lawrence College in Bronxville, New York, where he continued acting, starring in shows such as Kenneth Lonergan's "This Is Our Youth." He also used the school's freeform class structure to his advantage, expanding his horizons and stepping behind the camera, and wrote and directed "NIGHT NIGHT," his first short film, which now has over 170k views on YouTube.

What's New

Rohan's most recent directorial endeavor, "The Whistler," just premiered at the RISEFLIX Exclusive Showcase at the historic AMT Theater in the heart of Manhattan, where it was screened alongside various other films, including the award-winning short "Black Hat."
